Mission Statement

The client wishes for the website to achieve several goals. The client needs the website to recruit new members and increase the engagement of all members in the TCMC's activities. In terms of cencerts, the website aims to increase the number of tickets sold, as well as to encorage potential performers to let the TCMC organise their concerts and engage in promoting their own events (Through photo shoots/ media releases).

Success Evaluation

The success of the website can be evaluated by noting the number of new members that sign up with the TCMC, as well as the number of concert tickets sold in the three months before the website is upgraded. After the newly upgraded website has been online for 3 months, the number of new memberships sold, and the number of concert tickets purchased should be compared to the three months before the launch of the website upgrade. If the number of new members enrolled and the number of tickets sold is significantly greater in the months after the upgrade, the website has achieved the intended goal.

Target Audience

The current audience, whom frequently attends concerts held by the TCMC are usually men and women who fall in the age demographic of above 40. These audience members are also very strict on what music types they enjoy and are very resistant to change.

The client wishes to 'specialise in diversity'; thus holding a range of different concert genres. Because current members fall in the above 40 age bracket and are already attending concerts, in order to sell more tickets and sign-up new members, the website will be aimed at a younger age demographic. In particular the website will aim for people between the ages of 20 and 35. This new audience will enjoy a range of music genre and are perhaps less decided on one specific genre or concert type that they enjoy.

Aiming at a younger target audience hopes to attract a number of new members that will volunteer in their spare time to assist with concert preparations, as well as to ensure that the TCMC sticks around for generations of music lovers to come.

Content

Home Page
  • Brief Paragraph about the activities that the music centre organises e.g. Live in-house music.
  • Image slider with photoshoped images containing details of upcoming events
Events Page
  • Scrollable page of upcoming events
  • Click on an event to display details about the event and to purchase tickets to the event.
Bulletin Board
  • A community board where users can sign-up and post notes and events
  • Event listing will include: artist name, brief description about genre and artist group, concert time and date, and links to buy tickets to the concert.
Apply Page
  • The apply page will highlight the benefits of becoming a member of the TCMC
  • There will be a number of 'sub' pages, including 'Become a member', 'Perform with the TCMC' and 'Volunteer with the TCMC'. These pages will be a form that enables the user to submit information relevant to what they are signing up for, and provide any information that they should be aware of.
Local Talent Page
  • Will have all artist pictures in columns, and band names as well as a brief paragraph about their genre.
  • When a user clicks on an individual artists picture this will link them to information about the specific artist/group, as well as their contact details.
About Us page
  • Contact information for the TCMC: Phone 07 4724 2086, Mobile 0402 255 182 ,Postal Address: PO Box 1006, Townsville, Qld 4810, Address: Townsville Civic Theatre, 41 Boundary Street, Townsville, Qld 4810, email: admin@townsvillemusic.org.au
  • Information about the sponsors of the TCMC (Townsville City Council)
  • Will include a brief history of the TCMC over the years (content from the original pages).
Feeder Page: Health benefits of music
  • Will have a list of health benefits accociated with listening to music, encouraging people to become a member and attend some concerts held by the TCMC.
