Mission Statement

The main goal of the website is to attract new members and volunteers. As well as to get these new and current members to engage more in events, concerts and activities held by the TCMC.

Success Evaluation

The success of the Website can be evaluated by noting how many new sign-ups the center has in the three months before the website is upgraded. After newly created website has been online for 3 months, the number of member sign-ups should be compared to the three months before the website was launched. If the number of sign-ups in the 3 months is significantly greater, the website has achieved the intended goal.

Target Audience

The current audience that frequently attends concerts with TCMC are usually in the age demographic of above 40. The current audience are also very strict on what music types they enjoy. In order to widen the target audience and attract more members; the website is to be aimed at younger generations. This will shift the target demographic to people whom may attend concerts more regularly, thus boosting ticket sales. As well as ensure that the TCMC stays alive for generations to come.

Content

Home Page
  • Brief Paragraph about the activities that the music centre organises.
  • Links to the Events page and to become a member (client goal orentated).
  • Image slider with photoshoped images with details of upcoming events
Events Page
  • Scrollable page of upcoming events
  • Click on an event to display details about the specific artist
Bulletin Board
  • A community board where users can sign-up and post notes and events
  • Existing Bulletin Board posts will be included but restructured to suit the new layout
Apply Page
  • Will highlight the benefits of being a member and encourage users to sign-up!
  • Will have details to become a performer with the TCMC and provide forms that need to be submitted to hold a concert.
  • Will have forms to submit in order to become a member of the TCMC.
Local Talent Page
  • Will have all artist pictures in columns, and band names.
  • When a user clicks on an individual artists picture this will link them to information about the specific artist/group, as well as their contact details.
