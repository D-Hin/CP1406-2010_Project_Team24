Health benefits of listening to Music

Physical benefits:

As may music lovers may know, listening to music can be good for the soul. It can assist in calming nerves, easing stress, giving your energy levels a boost, as well as bringing back memories and stimulating emotions!

However, reasearch shows that music can have other beneficial affects on your health! Here are 10 reasons you should listen to more music:


1. Ease Pain

Music has been proven to reduce the perceived intensity of pain. Specifically in palliative care patients and other areas of helthcare that focuses on relieving patient suffering.


2. Motivate and improve atheletic performance

Listening to a few tunes can dramatically improve workout motivation and endurance. Music is said to work through the power of distraction; when you're belting out the chorus to your favourite song, you might not notice you ran that extra kilometre!


3. Speed up post-workout recovery

Listening to a few tunes after a workout, can help the body recover faster, studies have proven.


4. Improve Sleep quality

For all you classical music lovers, classical music has been shown to effectively treat insomnia in college students. Making music a safer, cheaper alternative to medications!


5. Enhance blood vessel function

Tests carried out on hospital patients have shown that listening to music has a healthy effect on blood vessel function. Participants reported feeling happier and resulted in an increas of blood flow within their blood vessels.


Mental Benefits:

6. Reduce Stress

Research shows that listening to music can relieve stress by triggering biochemical stress reducers.


7. Create a meditative state

Listening to slower music can alter brainwave speed; creating brainwave activity similar to that of meditation. Inturn, easing symptoms of migraines, PMS and behavioural issues.


8. Relieve depression

When feeling down, listening to some classical and meditive sounds has been proven helpful in elevating mood. Heavy metal and techno music can make depressive symptoms worse, so stay away from these if you are down!


9. Improve cognitive performance

Background music may enhance performance when carrying out cognative tasks. Studies have found that listening to music whilst completeing a test allowed those tested to complete more questions in the time period, and answer more correctly.


10. Reduce Anxiety

Listening to music can be as effective to reduce anxiety as getting a massage. Music has also helped cancer patients to communicate their feelings, manage stress and ease discomfort.


Clearly just listening to music can have a huge benefit for both your physical health and your mental state. All the more reason to listen to your favourite album on repeat!


Now that you have seen the benefits of music on your health, why not join us at the Townsville Music Centre...It is good for your health!
